Harrison Garcia

Photography was introduced  in the year of 1827 when Joseph Nicéphore Niépce, a French man from the region of Burgundy, took a photo of the landscape outside of his estate. The art has evolved over the years, transforming from a hobby of the few to a habit of the common person. The majority of the work I produced in my junior year of high school has been black and white film photography, with a slight exploration of digital photography.  Please use the link below to view my photographs.
